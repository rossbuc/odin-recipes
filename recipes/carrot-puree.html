Carrot Puree

This is a great side for any dinner party main you have planned for the evening, it's quick, easy, and delicious! There are numerous flavourings and variations you can add to this dish such as truffle oil or infusing the carrots with star anise when boiling them.

To get a really silky smooth puree you could pass it through a fine siv, but to get things done quickly you can just stick it in a food processor and blend it for a while until smooth, or use a hand blander to the same effect.

Ingredients:

Serves 4

  • Carrots 400g
  • Butter 100g
  • Nutmeg 1tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Chop the carrots into small cylinders.
  2. Add to a pan of boiling water along with the nutmeg and allow to cook for 15-20 mins, or until the carrots are soft.
  3. Strain the carrots and add to a food processor, blend untill smooth slowly adding the butter.
