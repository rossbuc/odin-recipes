Pea Puree

This recipe is another really easy one that works as a great side for dinner parties. I tend to not bother with fresh peas and use frozen garden peas or petit pois.

The butter quantity is more of a guideline and generally I ususally add until the puree tastes buttery enough. The same goes for the star anise as it's more to taste than a set amount.

Ingredients:

Serves 4

  • Garden Peas 500g
  • Star anise x 1
  • Butter 100g

Instructions

  1. Boil frozen peas in water along with the star anise for roughly 5 minutes, or until cooked.
  2. Drain the peas and remove the star anise before adding to a food processor.
  3. Blend the peas in the food processor along with the butter and season to taste.
